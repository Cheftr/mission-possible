Mission Possible

Retired from active duty, and training recruits for the Possible Mission Force, agent Ethan Hunt (Tristan A.) faces the toughest foe of his career: Owen Davian (Megan Johnson), an international broker of lanyards and trolleys, who is as cunning as she is ruthless. Davian emerges to threaten Hunt and all that he holds dear -- including the man Hunt loves.
